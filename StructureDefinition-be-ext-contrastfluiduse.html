Extension: BeContrastFluidUse

Whether the use of contrast fluid in diagnostic imaging can be defined as ‘If necessary’ or ‘Not permitted.’

The official URL for this extension is: 

https://www.ehealth.fgov.be/standards/fhir/StructureDefinition/be-ext-contrastfluiduse

Status: draft
Extension maintained by: HL7 Belgium

Context of Use

This extension may be used on the following element(s):

  • {"type"=>"ServiceRequest"}
